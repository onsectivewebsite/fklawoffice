Meet Your Counsel

Iqra Tahreem, Barrister & Solicitor

A member of both the Law Society of Ontario (2023) and the Pakistan Bar Association (2017), Iqra brings a unique international perspective to every file. She has appeared before the Superior Court of Justice and the Ontario Court of Justice, and has collaborated with Canadian law firms and the judiciary to sharpen her litigation and drafting craft.

As an immigrant herself, Iqra offers more than representation — she offers understanding. Her dual-jurisdictional insight allows her to serve clients from diverse cultural and legal backgrounds with confidence and clarity.

Working With Us

From first call to final resolution.

A clear, predictable process — so you know what's next at every stage of your matter.

01

Confidential Consultation

We listen first. You share your circumstances — in your preferred language — and we outline your legal options, realistic outcomes, and likely costs in plain terms.

02

Strategy & Retainer

A written plan with timelines, documents required, fees, and likelihood of success. No decision is made before you fully understand it.

03

Preparation & Advocacy

Evidence gathered, documents drafted, applications filed, hearings prepared. We handle the heavy lifting while keeping you informed at each milestone.

04

Resolution & Next Steps

Final agreement, order, judgment, or decision — followed by guidance on what comes next, from filing to implementation.
