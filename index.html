Meet Your Counsel

Iqra Tahreem, Barrister & Solicitor

A member of both the Law Society of Ontario (2023) and the Pakistan Bar Association (2017), Iqra brings a unique international perspective to every file. She has appeared before the Superior Court of Justice and the Ontario Court of Justice, and has collaborated with Canadian law firms and the judiciary to sharpen her litigation and drafting craft.

As an immigrant herself, Iqra offers more than representation — she offers understanding. Her dual-jurisdictional insight allows her to serve clients from diverse cultural and legal backgrounds with confidence and clarity.
