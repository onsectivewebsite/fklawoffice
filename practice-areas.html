Our Practice Areas

Boutique, focused legal services — delivered with the rigour of a large firm and the warmth of a trusted advisor.

01 — Family Law

Family Law

Strategic, compassionate representation in Ontario family matters.

Iqra has a deep commitment to helping families navigate some of life's most challenging legal issues. Her family law practice in Ontario includes assisting clients with divorce, custody and access disputes, child and spousal support, parenting plans, separation agreements, and property division under the Family Law Act and Divorce Act.

She is known for her compassionate yet strategic advocacy, always working to protect her clients' rights and promote the best interests of children. Iqra has appeared before the Superior Court of Justice and the Ontario Court of Justice on a range of family files.

  • Divorce & Uncontested Divorce
  • Custody, Access & Parenting Plans
  • Child Support
  • Spousal Support
  • Separation Agreements
  • Property & Equalization
  • Domestic Contracts
  • Motions & Court Applications
  • Enforcement Proceedings
  • Best Interests of the Child
02 — Immigration Law

Immigration Law

Thoughtful guidance for newcomers, students, workers, and families.

Iqra's immigration services offer thoughtful, dependable support to individuals and families adjusting to life in a new country. As an immigrant herself, she brings a deeply personal understanding of the challenges, uncertainties, and hopes that come with starting over in an unfamiliar place.

Our practice spans the full spectrum of Canadian immigration — from temporary visas and student permits to permanent residence, sponsorship, and appeals. We approach each file with patience, cultural awareness, and a clear-eyed view of your options.

  • Permanent Residence Applications
  • Express Entry Guidance
  • Spousal & Family Sponsorship
  • Work Permits & LMIA
  • Study Permits
  • Visitor Visas & Extensions
  • Humanitarian & Compassionate Applications
  • Appeals & Judicial Review
  • Citizenship Applications
  • Settlement Support & Documentation
03 — Refugee Law

Refugee Law

Dignified, patient support for those seeking safety and a fresh start.

Iqra works closely with refugees who have experienced displacement, loss, and prolonged uncertainty — providing careful guidance through asylum processes, documentation, and settlement needs. With patience, cultural awareness, and respect, Iqra helps refugees access essential services, reunite with family members, and begin rebuilding their lives in safety and stability.

Her lived experience allows her to connect on a human level, offering reassurance alongside practical support, and helping those seeking refuge move forward with dignity and confidence in their new community.

  • Refugee Claim Preparation
  • Basis of Claim (BOC) Forms
  • Immigration & Refugee Board Hearings
  • Pre-Removal Risk Assessment (PRRA)
  • Humanitarian & Compassionate Claims
  • Family Reunification
  • Settlement & Documentation
  • Appeals to the Refugee Appeal Division
  • Judicial Review at Federal Court
  • Access to Essential Services
04 — In Association

Real Estate Law

Residential real estate services offered by Sonia, working in association with FK Law Office.

In addition to our core practice areas, FK Law Office is pleased to connect clients with trusted real estate counsel. Sonia, working in association with our office, supports homebuyers, sellers, and families with residential real estate transactions across Ontario.

This collaboration lets our clients handle related matters — such as a property purchase following a separation, or settling into a new home after immigrating — with continuity and peace of mind.

  • Residential Purchases
  • Residential Sales
  • Title Transfers
  • Refinancing
  • Independent Legal Advice
  • Mortgage Documentation

Real estate services are provided by Sonia in association with FK Law Office. Please contact us for an introduction.
